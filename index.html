Refereeing App Project

During my time working as a soccer referee I developed this app in an effort to help with some of the problems faced during the job. A MAJOR problem especially during the outdoor season was keeping track of the exact score of games due to a lot of commotion during games Another problem was recording games as at times my bosses would ask me about the result of a game from a while ago. So this app allows keeping track of scores along with storing previous games with their dates and it provides a stopwatch that way everything a referee needs is in one place.
